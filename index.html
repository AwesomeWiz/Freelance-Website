Who We Are ?

ABOUT
WOODCO

Ahamad Ali Al Doubisi Contracting est. (Woodco) is a leading industrial supplier in Saudi Arabia offering a diverse range of products and services to different industries. The company specializes in manufacturing high quality wooden Packing, in addition to industrial supplies, outfit uniforms, PC solar mountings and solar panels. With a focus on meeting customer needs and international standards. Woodco has expanded its services to include oil and gas, chemical industries, manufacturing companies, hospitals, pharmaceutical companies, etc. Since 1995, Woodco has build a reputation as a trustworthy and reliable supplier known for its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

We derive our legacy and leadership from our history that lasts for more than quarter of a century, focused with the Industrial supplies, Wood manufacturing and Uniforms sector. Now we are one of the major suppliers of Aramco and other retail markets. There is and integrated team of experts and competent workers with planning and mastery to upgrade day by day in the industry and put it on the map of the major industrialized countries.
